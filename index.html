Also, there are some other important things to consider when buying an ice machine. The shape of frozen water will have the biggest impact on the quality of cooling of your food or your beverages, and the most common types of ice are cubes, nugget, crushed, and flaked ice. Cubes are the largest of these, and they can vary in shape and dimensions, but their biggest advantage is the fact that they offer maximum cooling, besides looking great in a whiskey glass. Nugget ice is smaller than cubes, has a softer structure and can be easily chewed, which makes it suitable for use in healthcare and medical purposes. Crushed and flaked ice are very similar in shape and structure, and only the smallest of differences separate these two types apart. They are very soft, small, and they melt very quickly so due to their softness, the food industry is a big consumer of these types of ice.
